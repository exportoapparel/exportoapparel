About Us

Who We Are

ExPorto is a trusted name in global garment exports, specializing in 100% original branded stocklot garments and customized fresh production for international buyers.

With a robust factory network and deep market knowledge, we connect Europe, America, and beyond to Bangladesh’s best apparel — with transparency, efficiency, and commitment.

Our Mission

To deliver top-quality branded stock garments and tailored sourcing services — built on trust, speed, and consistent excellence.

Our Vision

To be the global go-to sourcing partner — linking buyers with the most reliable factories and fashion solutions in Bangladesh.

Our Working Process

1. Buyer Inquiry

Understanding product requirements from buyer.

2. Product Sourcing

Stocklot search or factory coordination based on needs.

3. Sample Approval

Sending samples to buyer and confirming quality.

4. Production / Inspection

Fresh order execution or final QC on stock products.

5. Documentation & Shipment

On-time delivery with export compliance documents.

6. After Sales Support

Assisting buyers even after shipment is done.
